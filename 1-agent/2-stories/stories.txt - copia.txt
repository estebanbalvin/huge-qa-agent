Ticket id : Jira-413
User Story: New Employee Registration

URL: https://testing1.geekqa.net/

Title: Registration of collaborators in the GeekRetail system.

As a: Human Resources Administrator.
I want to: Enter the personal, contact, and employment data of a new employee into the registration form.
So that: I can maintain an updated and centralized database of the company's personnel.

Acceptance Criteria:
Mandatory Fields and Validation: The system must allow the entry of:

Personal Information: Name (min. 3 characters), Age (between 18 and 65 years), Date of Birth, and Gender.

Contact: Address, Email (valid format), and Phone Number.

Employment Data: Position, Department (Sales, Administration, Logistics), Contract Status (Active/Inactive), Schedule, Profile URL, and Salary.

Error Validation: If the user attempts to register data that does not meet the rules (e.g., age out of range or invalid email), the system must display a specific error message under the corresponding field.

Registration Confirmation: Upon clicking "Register Employee" and passing validations, the message "Empleado registrado exitosamente!" (Employee registered successfully!) must be displayed.

Table Visualization: The data of the newly registered employee must immediately appear in the "Registered Employees" table at the bottom of the page.

Atencion: No correr pruebas de accessibilidad sobre esta pagina.
